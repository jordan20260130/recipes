# Steamed Jasmine Rice

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Great Value Jasmine (Thai Hom Mali) rice
  - UPC: 0 78742 05301 1 (5 lb bag)

## Equipment

- Pot with measurement markings
- Scale (optional but recommended)

## Measurements by Weight

| State | Weight |
|-------|--------|
| Empty pot | 465 g |
| Pot + rice | 755 g |
| Pot + rice + water | 1367 g |
| **Water added** | **612 g** |

## Water Level Method

Fill water to the **2.25 mark** on the pot (with rice already in).

## Instructions

1. Rinse rice until water runs clear (optional, removes excess starch)
2. Add rice to pot
3. Add water to 2.25 mark (or add 612 g water by weight)
4. Bring to a boil
5. Reduce heat to low, cover tightly
6. Steam for 15-18 minutes
7. Remove from heat, let rest 5 minutes covered
8. Fluff with fork and serve

## Notes

- Thai Hom Mali = Thai Jasmine rice
- Water ratio by volume: approximately 1.3 cups water per cup rice
- Water ratio by weight: approximately 2.1 g water per g rice (612 g water for 290 g rice)
